Decision Task

~10 minutes

In this task you will make a series of choices between two boxes containing purple and yellow balls.

Box K has a known composition — you can see exactly how many balls are purple and how many are yellow.

Box U has an unknown composition — the proportion of purple and yellow balls is not shown.

On each trial, one ball will be drawn from the box you choose. If a purple ball is drawn, you win.
